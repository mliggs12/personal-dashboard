# DSTP Business Context

## Company Overview

**Company Name:** Don't Surrender To Pain (DSTP)

**Tagline:** "In Strength and Wisdom, We Overcome"

## Mission

To empower and inspire individuals on their journey towards resilience and well-being, guided by the enduring principles of Stoicism. We're dedicated to transforming lives through holistic support, authentic wisdom, and a community-driven approach, making a healthier, more fulfilling life accessible to all.

## Focus Areas

- Overcoming addiction
- Mental and physical well-being
- Embracing the stoic journey towards resilience

## Content Types

- Blog posts (long-form, in-depth content)

- Tweets
- Conference talks
- Workshops
